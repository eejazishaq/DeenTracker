# Islamic Habit Tracker & Salah Companion

## Business Objective

Help Muslims improve prayer consistency, build Islamic habits, and monitor spiritual growth through a local-first mobile application.

## Target Users

- Students
- Professionals
- Families
- Travelers

- Multiple calculation methods
- Current prayer
- Next prayer
- Countdown

### Notifications
- Per-prayer reminders
